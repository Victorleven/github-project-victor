Our Travelers' Top Three Destinations This Year

Every traveler dreams of finding that perfect place—where adventure, culture, and unforgettable memories come together. As the year comes to a close, our community of explorers has shared their favorite destinations that captured their hearts and imaginations. From hidden gems to iconic landmarks, these top three destinations represent the very best of what travel has to offer.

In this article, we’re diving into the stories, tips, and experiences that make these places a must-visit for any traveler. Whether you’re planning your next getaway or simply dreaming of far-off lands, let these destinations inspire your wanderlust.

1. Norwegian Fjords: A Land of Epic Landscapes and Thrilling Adventures

There’s something magical about the Norwegian fjords—a blend of serene beauty and raw wilderness that makes them feel almost otherworldly. For travelers seeking adventure and stunning vistas, this region offers an experience like no other.

One of the fjords' iconic highlights is the hike to Trolltunga (the Troll’s Tongue), a rock formation that juts out dramatically over Lake Ringedalsvatnet. The trek is no casual stroll; it’s a challenging 20-kilometer round trip that demands stamina, but every step rewards you with breathtaking scenery—snow-capped peaks, cascading waterfalls, and the fjords’ deep blue waters stretching into the distance. The real magic happens when you reach the summit. Standing on Trolltunga feels like standing on the edge of the world. It’s equal parts exhilarating and humbling, a moment where nature’s grandeur leaves you speechless. And let’s not forget that perfect photo op, where you dangle your feet off the cliff and capture the essence of adventure.

Beyond Trolltunga, the fjords offer countless opportunities to explore. You can kayak along tranquil waters, visit charming coastal villages, or hop aboard a ferry to witness these majestic landscapes from a whole new perspective. For those who crave solitude, there’s something deeply calming about standing in the midst of nature this vast and untouched.

The Norwegian fjords aren’t just a destination; they’re an experience—a place where adventure seekers and nature lovers alike can connect with something greater than themselves. If you’re ready to lace up your hiking boots and embrace the call of the wild, the fjords are calling your name.

2. Santorini, Greece: A Sun-Drenched Escape Full of Charm and Wonder

Santorini is a destination that seems pulled straight from a dream, with its iconic whitewashed buildings cascading down cliffs and endless views of the shimmering Aegean Sea. For travelers seeking a mix of relaxation, culture, and stunning scenery, this Greek island offers a perfect slice of paradise.

The charm of Santorini lies in its layers—both literally and figuratively. Start your journey by exploring the narrow, winding streets of Oia, where every turn reveals postcard-perfect views of blue-domed churches and bougainvillea-covered terraces. Time your visit for sunset, when the sky transforms into a canvas of fiery orange and soft pinks, and the entire town feels alive with magic. For those who crave adventure, a hike along the famous Fira to Oia trail offers a unique way to experience the island’s beauty. The 10-kilometer path winds past rugged cliffs, quaint villages, and stunning vistas of the caldera, giving you a more intimate connection to Santorini’s dramatic landscape.

But Santorini isn’t just about views—it’s about the moments you create. Sipping local Assyrtiko wine at a cliffside taverna, indulging in fresh seafood by the water, or diving into the crystal-clear sea from the volcanic beaches creates memories that linger long after you’ve left. Whether you’re wandering ancient ruins in Akrotiri, sailing across the caldera on a catamaran, or simply savoring the slow pace of island life, Santorini has a way of captivating the soul. It’s a place that feels timeless yet vibrant, romantic yet adventurous—a destination where every traveler can find their own piece of bliss.

3. New Orleans: A City of Soul, Flavor, and Celebration

New Orleans isn’t just a destination—it’s an experience, a living, breathing mosaic of cultures, sounds, and flavors. For travelers, it’s a city that ignites the senses and invites you to revel in its rhythm, charm, and unapologetic zest for life.

Strolling through the historic French Quarter, you’re immediately immersed in the city’s unique personality. The wrought-iron balconies, cobblestone streets, and colorful Creole cottages whisper stories of centuries past. Street performers bring the air alive with music, and the scent of beignets and fresh coffee from Café du Monde tempts you to pause for a sweet treat. For music lovers, New Orleans is pure heaven. From the soulful jazz pouring out of clubs on Frenchmen Street to the lively brass bands in the streets of Tremé, music isn’t just entertainment here—it’s a way of life. Grab a seat in a local venue, and you’ll quickly understand why this city is considered the birthplace of jazz.

Food in New Orleans is an adventure in itself. A bowl of gumbo or jambalaya tells a story of Creole and Cajun heritage, while po’boys and fresh oysters offer a taste of the city’s more casual, flavorful side. And don’t miss the chance to savor a festive king cake or pralines for dessert. Every bite feels like a celebration.

Beyond the indulgences, there’s a deep history to explore. A visit to the hauntingly beautiful St. Louis Cemetery or the National WWII Museum offers glimpses into the city’s rich, multifaceted past. And for those who love a touch of mystery, the tales of ghosts and voodoo culture woven into the fabric of New Orleans are sure to intrigue.

New Orleans is a city that leaves its mark on every traveler who walks its streets. It’s vibrant, soulful, and alive—a place where the past and present blend seamlessly, creating a world like no other. Here, every moment feels like a celebration, and visitors quickly learn what the locals mean when they say, “laissez les bon temps rouler”—let the good times roll.
